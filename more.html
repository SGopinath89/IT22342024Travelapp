    World's End

    World's End is located within the Horton Plains National Park in Nuwara Eliya District, Sri Lanka. It is a sheer cliff, with a drop of about 4,000 feet. It is one of the most visited parts of the Park, and a key tourist attraction in the Nuwara Eliya District and the country at large
